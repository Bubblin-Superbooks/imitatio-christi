Even for the diversity of season different exercises are needed, some suit better for feasts, some for fasts. We need one kind in time of temptations and others in time of peace and quietness. Some are suitable to our times of sadness, and others when we are joyful in the Lord.
  2. When we draw near the time of the great feasts, good exercises should be renewed, and the prayers of holy men more fervently besought.
