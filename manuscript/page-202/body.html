Chapter Vi

Of the proving of the true lover

"My Son, thou art not yet strong and prudent in thy love."

  1. Wherefore, O my Lord?
  2. "Because for a little opposition thou fallest away from thy undertakings, and too eagerly seekest after consolation. The strong lover standeth fast in temptations, and believeth not the evil persuasions of the enemy. As in prosperity I please him, so in adversity I do not displease.
