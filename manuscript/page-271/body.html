Chapter Xxiii

Of four things which bring great peace

"My Son, now will I teach thee the way of peace and of true liberty."

  1. Do, O my Lord, as Thou sayest, for this is pleasing unto me to hear.
  2. "Strive, My Son, to do another's will rather than thine own. Choose always to have less rather than more. Seek always after the lowest place, and to be subject to all. Wish always and pray that the will of God be fulfilled in thee. Behold, such a man as this entereth into the inheritance of peace
